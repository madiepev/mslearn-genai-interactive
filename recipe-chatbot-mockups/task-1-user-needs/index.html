Task 1: Identify User Needs

Select the 3 most relevant user needs for a supermarket recipe chatbot from the 9 options below.

Scenario Context

Supermarket: FreshMart - a family-friendly grocery chain with focus on fresh ingredients and healthy eating

Customer Base: Busy families, health-conscious shoppers, and cooking enthusiasts

Chatbot Goal: Help customers discover recipes, plan meals, and make informed food choices

Recipe Search & Discovery
Find recipes based on ingredients, dietary preferences, or cooking time constraints
Price Comparison
Compare prices of similar products across different brands and sizes
Nutritional Information
Get detailed nutrition facts, allergen information, and health benefits of ingredients
Store Navigation
Find product locations and get directions within the physical store
Meal Planning
Plan weekly meals, create shopping lists, and organize family dining schedules
Cooking Tips & Techniques
Learn cooking methods, ingredient substitutions, and kitchen tips for better results
Inventory Management
Track home pantry items, expiration dates, and suggest recipes based on what you have
Delivery Tracking
Monitor order status, delivery times, and manage pickup/delivery preferences
Seasonal Suggestions
Discover seasonal produce, holiday recipes, and timely cooking recommendations
0 of 3 needs selected

Choose exactly 3 user needs that are most relevant for a recipe-focused chatbot

Feedback

Correct Selection:

  1. Recipe Search & Discovery - Core functionality for a recipe chatbot
  2. Meal Planning - Natural extension of recipe assistance for families
  3. Cooking Tips & Techniques - Essential support for successful recipe execution

Why these needs matter:

  • Recipe Search is the primary function customers expect from a recipe chatbot
  • Meal Planning helps busy families organize their week and shopping
  • Cooking Tips ensure customers can successfully execute the recipes they find

Other needs: While important for a full supermarket app, they're less critical for a recipe-focused chatbot experience.
