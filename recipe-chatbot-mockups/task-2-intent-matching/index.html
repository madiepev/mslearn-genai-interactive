Intent 2: Meal Planning
Customer Query: "I need to plan healthy meals for my family this week. We have two teenagers and want to avoid processed foods."
Response A:
I'd be happy to help you plan healthy meals! For a family with teenagers, I recommend focusing on whole foods, lean proteins, and plenty of vegetables. Response B:
Check out our healthy eating section for meal ideas. We have lots of recipes that avoid processed ingredients.
Intent 3: Cooking Tips & Techniques
Customer Query: "Every time I cook salmon, it comes out dry. What am I doing wrong?"
Response A:
Salmon can be tricky to cook properly. Try our salmon recipes from the seafood section.
Response B:
Dry salmon is usually from overcooking! Here are key tips: **1)** Cook to 145°F internal temperature, **2)** Don't flip more than once, **3)** Try the "10-minute rule" - cook 10 minutes per inch of thickness, **4)** Consider baking in foil with lemon and herbs to retain moisture. Would you like me to suggest some foolproof salmon recipes from our collection?
