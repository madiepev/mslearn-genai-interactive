Task 3: Select Evaluation Metrics

Choose 3 Azure AI Foundry evaluation metrics that are most relevant for evaluating the recipe chatbot's performance.

Recipe Chatbot Evaluation Context

Business Goals:

  • Provide accurate recipe information and cooking guidance
  • Help customers discover relevant recipes based on their needs
  • Maintain helpful, friendly conversation that encourages cooking
  • Ensure responses are safe and appropriate for all family members

Success Criteria: High customer satisfaction, accurate information, relevant suggestions, and appropriate content

Groundedness
Accuracy Metric
Measures how well responses are supported by reliable sources and factual information
Best for: Recipe accuracy, cooking facts, nutritional information
Relevance
Quality Metric
Evaluates how well responses address the user's specific query and context
Best for: Recipe recommendations, personalized suggestions
Coherence
Quality Metric
Assesses how logical, clear, and well-structured the responses are
Best for: Recipe instructions, step-by-step guidance
Content Safety
Safety Metric
Detects harmful, inappropriate, or unsafe content in AI responses
Best for: Family-safe interactions, appropriate content
Similarity
Comparison Metric
Compares AI responses against reference answers or expected outputs
Best for: Standardized responses, template validation
Fluency
Language Metric
Measures grammatical correctness and natural language quality
Best for: Conversational quality, readability
