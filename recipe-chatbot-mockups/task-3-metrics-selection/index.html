Task 3: Select Evaluation Metrics

Choose 3 Azure AI Foundry evaluation metrics that are most relevant for evaluating the recipe chatbot's performance.

Recipe Chatbot Evaluation Context

Business Goals:

  • Provide accurate recipe information and cooking guidance using farm products
  • Help customers discover relevant recipes based on their needs and available ingredients
  • Maintain helpful, friendly conversation that encourages cooking with farm products
  • Ensure responses are safe and appropriate for all family members

Success Criteria: High customer satisfaction, accurate information, relevant suggestions, and appropriate content

Available Metrics: Select from Azure AI Foundry's built-in evaluation metrics across AI Quality, Risk & Safety categories

Groundedness
AI Quality (AI Assisted)
Evaluates how well responses are supported by the provided context or source material
Best for: Recipe accuracy, cooking facts, ingredient information
Relevance
AI Quality (AI Assisted)
Measures how well responses address the user's specific query and intent
Best for: Recipe recommendations, personalized cooking suggestions
Coherence
AI Quality (AI Assisted)
Assesses how logical, clear, and well-structured the responses are
Best for: Recipe instructions, step-by-step cooking guidance
Fluency
AI Quality (AI Assisted)
Measures grammatical correctness and natural language quality of responses
Best for: Conversational quality, readability of instructions
GPT Similarity
AI Quality (AI Assisted)
Compares AI responses against ground truth or reference answers using semantic similarity
Best for: Standardized recipe responses, consistent information
Self-harm Related Content
Risk and Safety
Detects content that could promote or encourage self-harmful behaviors
Best for: Ensuring safe cooking advice, appropriate dietary guidance
Hateful and Unfair Content
Risk and Safety
Identifies discriminatory, biased, or hateful content in responses
Best for: Inclusive, respectful cooking recommendations for all customers
Violent Content
Risk and Safety
Detects content that describes or promotes violence
Best for: Family-safe cooking environment, appropriate content
Sexual Content
Risk and Safety
Identifies inappropriate sexual content in AI responses
Best for: Family-appropriate cooking conversations
